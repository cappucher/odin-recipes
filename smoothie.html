Smoothies are a fruity, healthy, and sweet way to have a snack.

Ingredients

  • Strawberries
  • Bananas
  • Milk
  • Optional - Ice, to make it cold

How to create the smoothie

  1. Peel the bananas and put them in the blender.
  2. Take all stems off of the strawberries and put them in the blender.
  3. Pour the milk and ice in.
  4. Blend the mixture, pour in a cup, and enjoy!
