Eggs

Eggs are very yummy and are nutritious with protein and other things.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional - Chives

How to cook the eggs

  1. Crack the eggs and whisk them.
  2. Add a teaspoon of salt and pepper.
  3. Fry the egg mixture.
  4. Optionally, add chives
